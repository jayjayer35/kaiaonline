below i have embedded videos, linked to the youtube videos that i have posted, you should def check them out if you have the time to spare.

make sure to pause/mute the site music if you watch the videos here!!

first year | 2022-2023

I was rollerblading around Miami Beach, and it hit me, I just knew this had to be the song.
